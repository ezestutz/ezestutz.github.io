"Sugerencias y Reclamos" - Helpdesk

Another project in the internship at the town hall of Capitán Sarmiento. It's a helpdesk based in osTicket, with profund structural and stylish modifications, done in PHP (with SQL querys), HTML, CSS and JavaScript, in order to adapt to the website's style and the town hall's needs. This porject was done with the purpose of creating a closer relationship between people and the government, and fit in the current situation of social isolation, so that people has no need to go physically to the town hall anymore.

The app has two faces. The fist one consist on a place where the user can open tickets with claims or suggestions and see the ones that he/she has already open; the second one is for the agents (those who are responsible to give response to this tickets). Once the ticket is open, a mail is sent to the agents with a link to sign in and answer.
This needed lessons for all 21 agents and also to other town hall workers, who are in touch with many people, so that they can teach them how to use the app "Atención al Vecino".

Theese information is visualized in an app running at the local police station, built with ReactJS and a backend with NodeJs and MySQL.
